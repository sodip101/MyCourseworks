How Technology Has Affected My Daily Life


Date: May 2, 2019


Humans have come a long way due to the advancement in technology. Tasks that were once very difficult to complete can be completed in minutes or even seconds. We cannot even imagine our day to day lives without some of the gadgets like smartphone, laptops, TVs, refridgerators, etc. that we have become so dependent upon.


As like any modern human being, I have become extremely dependent on the technology that I use everyday. I cannot even go a few hours without using my phone or my laptop. My life cannot function properly without my gadgets. I use my phone to check my social media and connect with my friends, I use it to stay updated with the current world events. I use my laptop to complete my assignments, I use it to watch movies. Both my work and entertainment are dependent upon a form of technology. Such is the impact of technology in my life.
